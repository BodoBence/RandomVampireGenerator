Sliders: The ratio of the sliders (mental, social, physical) in relation to eachother influences the "persoanlity" of the generated character. The Non-clan disciplines slider controls the opportunities to learn dark skills outside of one's clan.

Age: The maximum age is 3000, therefore in most cases the use of manual age is recommended for results that accomodate average gaming sessions.

Generation: These values range between 3 and 15, therefore a manual input will yield more usuable results in most cases, and avoids the creation of overpowering characters.

Level 6+ skills: VTM 5th edition currently gives disciplines only up to level 5. If needed higher level disciplines can be added using V20 rules.
