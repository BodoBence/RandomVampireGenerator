Sliders: The ratio of the focus sliders (mental, social, physical) in relation to each other influence the personality of the generated character. The "Non-clan disciplines" slider controls the ratio to which the character might learn dark skills outside of their clan.

Age: The maximum age is 3000, therefore in most cases the use of manual age is recommended for characters that accomodate average gaming sessions better.

Generation: These values range between 3 and 16, therefore, a manual input will yield more usuable characters in most cases, and avoids the creation of overpowering antediluvians.

Level 5+ skills: VTM 5th edition currently describes disciplines only up to level 5. If needed, higher level disciplines can be added using V20 rules.
